Rules

All VIPs have been given their title because of their involvement in the community and their activity in the game.

Please remember, however, that with power comes responsibility. You should not mic spam, especially when it means speaking over the commander or interrupting the current day.

Players should only be revived before the day begins (which is when the cell doors open). A couple of seconds after is fine, too, if it's taking you a while to revive players or the player to be revived joined before doors opened.

Reviving a player who has been killed by a door glitch is okay, unless that player was purposely glitching the map (e.g. trying to escape out the window of jb_asylum2_ag and was killed in the process).
