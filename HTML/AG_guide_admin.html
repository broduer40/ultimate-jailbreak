Allied-Gamers.com - Admin Guide

Fo sho'

While the things listed below are general recommendations on how to approach issues - admins should use your own judgement before anything else. Afterall, we've all gotten our admin status because we're experience on this server and the community trusts us.

Always use the minimum amount of power necessary to resolve an issue. If, for example, a player is disrespecting members, and asking the player to stop may resolve the problem, that's preferred over gagging or kicking.

While we allow swearing on the server, and most of us tolerate the occasional derogatory term, our members (especially the young ones) may be easily offended or influenced. They should never be expected to "get used to" hateful language. This may only be a game, but it still affects the mindset and the language that young members use outside of it.

Members may not always feel safe speaking up for themselves. It's our responsibility to check in with them when we think someone else has taken things too far. Make sure they know we've got their backs.

When warnings/gags/kicks don't solve a problem, sometimes even a 10 minute ban won't work. If banning for multiple days, you'll need to specify the time in minutes. 1500 minutes = 25 hours.

    AMX Commands

    Base Admin Commands

  • amx_kick [name/userid] [reason] - Kick a player from the server.
  • amx_ban [name/userid] [time] [reason] - Ban a player from the server for a specified number of minutes.
  • amx_addban [steamid/ip] [time] [reason] - Just like amx_ban, but can be used even after the player leaves the server.
  • amx_unban [steamid/ip] - Removes a player's ban.
  • amx_slay [name/userid] - Slay a player.
  • amx_map [mapname] - Change the map.
  • amx_nick [name] [new name] - Change a player's name.
  • amx_last - View the names and steamIDs of recently-disconnected players.

    • Chat Commands

  • amx_psay [name/userid] [message] - Send a private message to a player.
  • amx_tsay [color] [message] - Sends a left side HUD message to all players.
  • amx_csay [color] [message] - Sends a center HUD message to all players.

    • Vote Commands

  • amx_votemap [map] [map] [map] [map] - Starts a vote for a map.
  • amx_vote [question] [answer#1] [answer#2] - Starts a custom poll.
  • amx_cancelvote - Cancels the current poll.

    • Extra Commands

  • amxmodmenu - Shows the full AMX Super menu.
  • amx_transfer [name] [T/CT/SPEC] - Transfer a player to a certain team.
  • amx_gravity [amount] - Sets the server gravity.
  • amx_gag [name/userid/steamid] - Gags a player from speaking.
  • amx_ungag [name/userid/steamid] - Ungags a player.
  • amx_disarm [name/userid/steamid/@team] - Disarm a player or an entire team.
  • amx_revive [name/userid/steamid/@team] - Revive a player or an entire team.
  • amx_godmode [name/userid/steamid/@team] [0/1]- Give/remove godmode.
  • amx_noclip [name/userid/steamid/@team] [0/1]- Give/remove noclip.

    • Ultimate Jailbreak Commands

  • uj_ban_guard [name/userid/steamID] [minutes] - Ban a player from joining the guard team.
